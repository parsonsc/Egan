Geoff Egan FRICS Managing Director
Geoff Egan has 32 years experience in property investment and property asset management. Founded Egan Property Asset Management Ltd in April 2010, formerly co-founded Egan Lawson in 1990 with Dougie Lawson with property assets under management of approximately £1bn. He heads the investment team which includes the Albemarle Syndicates established in 2001 and has developed the funds under asset management by Albemarle to over £350million.
Mark Parsons Finance Director
