Geoff Egan, originally co-founder of Egan Lawson over twenty years ago, established Egan Property Asset Management
(EPAM) in June 2010 as a specialist commercial property consultancy acting for the Albemarle Property Investment Syndicates
and private clients.
Our principal focus is to maximise returns by identifying and acquiring attractive investment opportunities and actively managing
the asset through to disposal.
Competitive fees and co-investment by the managing partners ensures a close alignment of interest. A solid relationship with a
number of major banks and building societies has led to finance being available on attractive terms.
 
Our aim is to provide an outstanding level of service using the extensive experience of our professional team.
