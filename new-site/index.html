Egan Property Asset Management

Geoff Egan founded Egan Lawson over twenty five years ago, and established Egan Property Asset Management (EPAM) in June 2010 as a specialist commercial property consultancy acting for the Albemarle Property Investment Syndicates, corporate and private clients. Our principal focus is to maximise returns from investment and occupational opportunities and to actively manage the asset through to disposal. Careful stock selection, active asset management and professional property management are the key elements to achieving our Clients objectives. Our aim is to provide an outstanding level of service using the extensive experience of our professional team.
